Research Mentoring

Jace Rusznak

2023- (co-supervised with Prof. Songhu Wang)

Jace is Indiana University's Department of Astronomy's inaugural Post-Baccalaureate researcher. Jace recently graduated from IU with a B.S. in Astronomy and Astrophysics in May of 2024. Jace is working on studying the orbital architectures of massive exoplanet systems through transit photometry, radial velocity, and Rossiter-McLaughlin measurements.

[1] Rusznak, J., Wang, X.-Y., Rice, M., et al. 2024, arXiv:2412.04438. Accepted by ApJL.

Jiamei Yang

2020-2021 (co-supervised with Prof. Kai Li)

Jiamei is currently a Ph.D. candidate at Beijing Normal University, working with Prof. Xing Wei. Jiamei is working on using N-body/hydrodynamical simulations to study exomoon migration in circumplanetary disks.

Yang, J.-M., Wang, X.-Y., Li, K., et al. 2021, PASJ, 73, 1010
